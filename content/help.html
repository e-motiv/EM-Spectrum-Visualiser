Help

Keys:

Mouse hoover:	Show info
Mouse click:	Lock info to the screen
Mousescroll:	Zoom scale or scroll text when text.
+:	Zoom in
-:	Zoom Out
0:	Zoom 100%
->:	Scroll Scale left
<-:	Scroll Scale right
You can customize and add data in several parts of this movie, such as allocations, applications, black bodies and attenuation, special frequencies, emission lines, ...
Everything works via XML files in the folder of the movie, starting with the menus "Menuallocation.xml, ...".
Have a look at them and make your own.  if you do, you are more than welcome to send them to me (Ruben@e-builds.com).  I will publish them all on the webspace of this movie.  Sharing is the future!  ;-)
(I am also considering any comments, feature requests...)
