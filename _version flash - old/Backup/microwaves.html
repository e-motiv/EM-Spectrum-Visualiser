MicroWaves or µW (1GHZ - 3000 GHZ)
Radio-frequencies are going higher and higher (due to better technology and crowdiness of lower frequencies), nowadays µwaves are also used for radiocommunication.

Sources: Stars, Sun

Uses: Microwave-oven (2.45 GHZ Water and fat absorb these frequencies and heat up) Radar (around 10 GHZ) At these frequencies the wave reflects on most objects, especially on metal surfaces. From the reflection and time-delay, detected at the radarstation, the distance, direction and surface of objects are generated. Communication (satellites; high capacity comm. devices)
